About Me:

Hello, my name is Jaclyn. I am a mathematician and computer programmer. In May of 2015 I graduated with my Bachelors and Master's in Mathematics from the University at Albany. I have been tutoring all levels of math from elementary school to college in all topics since 2013. I always had a strong passion for Algebra. Due to this my advisor suggested learning how to write code. In 2017 I decided to take a few Java courses at Hudson Valley Community College and loved it. I am currently taking a front-end web development class with Albany Can Code. My future goal is to become a software developer.


Personal Life
